About Missions

Missions are tasks you can complete to earn coins and experience points. There are different types of missions:

  • Daily Missions: Available every day, reset at midnight
  • Weekly Missions: Available for a week, reset on Sundays
  • Monthly Missions: Available for a month, reset on the 1st
  • Special Missions: One-time missions with special rewards

Complete missions to level up your character and earn coins to spend in the shop!
